About us

What we do

Birga provides a unified integration layer so your applications can talk to R-Keeper, Poster, and other POS systems through a single, consistent interface. We handle protocols, retries, and mapping so you can focus on your product.

Why it matters

Multiple POS integrations mean duplicated logic, different APIs, and maintenance overhead. Birga centralizes connectivity, reduces integration time, and makes it easy to add new POS providers as you grow.

Built for scale

Our gateway is designed for reliability and observability. We support modern auth, retries, and health checks out of the box.

FAQ

01 What is Birga?
Birga is a single integration gateway (SaaS) that lets your systems communicate with multiple POS providers through one API. Instead of integrating with each POS separately, you integrate once with Birga.
02 Which POS systems are supported?
We currently support R-Keeper and Poster, with more adapters in development. Contact us if you need a specific POS or delivery platform.
03 Can I use Birga on-premise?
We offer only cloud (SaaS) option for now. Our gateway is designed to be fully managed and scalable.
04 How do I get started?
